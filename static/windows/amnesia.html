Amnesia

Amnesia is a .dll library that allows for editing memory pointers of live-running applications. There are several other libraries and even apps for this however to my knowlege; none allow you to work with application modules in your pointers which really holds you back from a lot of processes. For example, if you're trying to manupulate a unity engine game, you might find your pointers preceded by "unityengine.dll" or "mono.dll". My library actually accounts for these, allowing you to manipulate a wide range of programs.
